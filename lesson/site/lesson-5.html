How to Play BitLife

BitLife is a life simulation game that lets you live an entire virtual life from birth to death. Every decision you make shapes your story — from your education and career to relationships, crime, fame, or fortune. The beauty of BitLife lies in its simplicity: all you do is choose, but every choice has consequences that can lead to wildly different outcomes.

Getting Started
When you start BitLife, you’re born as a random character in a random country. Each year, you’ll be presented with choices that determine your character’s fate. You can study, make friends, fall in love, get a job, commit crimes, or even become a celebrity. The possibilities are endless — no two lives are ever the same.

The game runs on a yearly progression system. Each time you tap “Age,” your character grows one year older, and new life events appear, such as school exams, illnesses, or relationship changes.

Controls
BitLife doesn’t require fast reactions — everything is menu-based. Simply tap on options to make decisions. Navigate through tabs like School, Relationships, Assets, Activities, and Jobs to manage different aspects of your character’s life. The interface is clean and text-driven, making it easy to explore all the features.

How to Play

  1. Start by focusing on education and health — smart and healthy characters have more opportunities.

  2. Build relationships with family and friends to increase happiness.

  3. Choose a career path that matches your goals — from doctor to pop star to criminal mastermind.

  4. Make choices carefully — bad decisions can lead to jail, bankruptcy, or even death.

  5. Live as long as possible and try different life paths to unlock achievements.

Tips and Strategy

  • Study hard early to unlock top-paying careers.

  • Keep your happiness, health, smarts, and looks balanced.

  • Avoid committing crimes unless you’re ready for risky outcomes.

  • Save money to buy houses and cars later in life.

  • Experiment with different lifestyles — each run tells a unique story.

Common Mistakes
Many new players ignore health and happiness, leading to early deaths. Others spend recklessly or commit crimes too soon. Remember, in BitLife, every action matters — one small choice can completely change your destiny.
