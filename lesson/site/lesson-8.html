Mr. Bullet – Think, Aim, and Fire!

In Mr. Bullet, every shot matters. You play as a secret agent with one goal — eliminate enemies using precision, creativity, and just a few bullets. It’s not about shooting wildly; it’s about thinking smart. Each level is a puzzle disguised as an action scene, where you must figure out the best angle and trajectory to take down your targets efficiently.

The gameplay is simple but deeply satisfying. You aim by dragging your finger or mouse to set the direction of your bullet, then release to fire. The challenge? Bullets bounce off walls, ceilings, and even obstacles — so you can pull off clever trick shots and defeat multiple enemies with a single bullet. Timing and geometry are key. A well-placed shot can ricochet across the screen and wipe out everyone before they even see it coming.

As you progress, the puzzles get trickier. You’ll face moving enemies, explosive barrels, and innocent hostages you must avoid hitting. Each level rewards creativity and precision rather than brute force. The fewer bullets you use, the higher your score and the more stars you earn. These stars unlock new missions, costumes, and hilarious skins that make the game even more enjoyable.

Mr. Bullet shines because it mixes casual fun with brain-teasing puzzles. You can play for a few minutes or lose yourself for hours, trying to master every level perfectly. Whether you’re a strategist or a sharpshooter, this game rewards style, accuracy, and quick thinking.

So get ready, agent — take aim, plan your shot, and show everyone why you’re called Mr. Bullet!
